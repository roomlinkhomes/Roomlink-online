Privacy Policy

RoomLink ("we", "us", "our") operates the RoomLink mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide directly: name, email address, phone number, profile photo, government-issued ID (for verification), payment method details (not stored by us), address, booking preferences.
  • Information about properties you list: photos, description, location, pricing, availability, house rules.
  • Automatically collected information: device information, IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, time spent, referral sources, location data (with permission), search queries, booking interactions.
  • Communications: messages sent through in-app chat, support tickets, emails.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service (including verification, matching, booking, payments, messaging)
  • Verify identity and property ownership/legality to prevent fraud
  • Process payments and send receipts/confirmations
  • Send service-related notifications, booking updates, security alerts
  • Enable communication between guests and hosts
  • Personalize your experience (recommendations, saved searches, favorites)
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Conduct analytics and research to improve our platform

3. Sharing Your Information

We share your information only in these limited cases:

  • With other users: necessary contact and booking details are shared between confirmed guests and hosts.
  • Service providers: payment processors, cloud storage, identity verification services, messaging infrastructure, analytics tools all under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: when required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • Business transfers: in the event of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. After account deletion, data is removed within 90 days except where retention is required by law.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent)
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@roomlink.homes.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
