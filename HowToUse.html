How to Use RoomLink

RoomLink is your go-to app for finding verified apartments and short-term stays. The app is not yet released but will be available soon on Google Play Store. Here's how it will work once launched:

Back to Home Download Coming Soon

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Download & Sign Up

Get the app from Google Play, create an account with email/phone, set up your profile (currency, language, dark/light mode, notifications preferences).

2

Search for Stays

Use powerful filters: location, price range, dates, amenities. View results on list or interactive map.

3

Browse & Favorite

See verified listings with photos, full details & host info. Save favorites for later.

4

Chat with Hosts

Message hosts directly in-app to ask questions or negotiate.

5

Book Securely

Select dates, review total (no hidden fees), pay safely via in-app wallet or payment gateway.

6

Manage Your Booking

Get instant confirmation, receive notifications, track check-in/out, view booking history.

7

Leave a Review

After your stay, rate the host/property and help the community.

8

For Hosts

List your property (goes through verification), manage bookings, chat with guests, withdraw earnings.

9

Support & Safety

Report issues, contact support, use our verification system for peace of mind.

Verified apartments & stylish short-term stays worldwide secure, transparent, effortless.
